Management System for HOTELS

This will make easy all the works from the proccess of managing all the information of your hotel staff and all the customers who come to the hotel, taking all the orders quickly and appropriately , placing the orders at correct time to the proccess of making final bills .

Top features in this system

All the features in this system is provided mainly for two catogories of people and they are different from each other as follows.

The speciallity of this system is the ability of getting the total cost from one click through a proper management of all the orders from the date the customer comes to the hotel up to the date he or she leaves the hotel. These specifications have been provided by us for the easiness of the small scale hotel owners like you.

Top features in this system

All the features in this system is provided mainly for two catogories of people and they are different from each other as follows.

1. Features that provide for the employees of the hotel.

2. Features that provide for the customers who comes to the hotel.

For the customers who comes to the hotel

Through this system, it can be easily done all the works that begings from booking a hotel room and end up with leaving the hotel . This system allows to get all the services that required to anyone by logging with the room number that has already entered to the system and it will proccess all the calculation untill the final bill value by managing all the details properly. As well as this allows customers to check their by themselves.

For the employees of the hotel

After recieving any order from a customer to the staff, all the information related to it such as what has ordered, which room has placed it, at what time it has been placed and what is the quantity of the order are provided for the dashboard in an appropriate manner. As well as if it is unable to provide any kind of service at the moment, system can adjust not to display it for the customer. Due to this, it doesn't require to aware the customer often. Moreover , as this system saves all the orders , it provides the ability to give a detailed information at the proccess of making the final bill.

System requirements

Processor: 1 gigahertz (GHz) or faster compatible processor
RAM: 1 gigabyte (GB)
Hard drive size: 2GB or larger hard disk
Graphics card: Compatible with DirectX 9 or later with WDDM 1.0 driver
Display: 800x600
